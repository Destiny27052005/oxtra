Africa Green future form (AGFF)

Shaping the Future of Clean Energy and Sustainable Innovation in Africa

The AGFF is a pan-African, non-profit sustainability platform founded and envisioned by Oxtra to accelerate Africa's transition to a green, inclusive, and climate-resilient future. AGFF is designed to build an ecosystem of businesses, innovators, and partners whose operations promote or align with sustainable practices. It is also an annual platform for green innovation, where we celebrate progress, showcase solutions, and push the boundaries of what's possible for Africa's green transition.

As the visionaries and conveners of AGFF, Oxtra leads the charge in aligning African governments, private sector leaders, innovators, youth, and development partners with both the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), especially SDGs 3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 12, 13, and 17 and the African Union's Agenda 2063, Africa's blueprint for inclusive growth and sustainable transformation

AGFF reflects Oxtra's deep commitment to accelerating Africa's transition to a low-carbon, inclusive future through policy advocacy, green entrepreneurship, clean transportation, and large-scale partnerships. It builds on our work in sustainable mobility and extends our impact to new sectors and nations across the continent.
